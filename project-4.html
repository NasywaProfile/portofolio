Project Overview

"LesAja" is an educational platform designed to bridge the gap between students and qualified teachers for both online and offline learning sessions. The website includes robust features such as a location and subject-based teacher search, detailed tutor biographies, and a dedicated user dashboard. This dashboard empowers students and parents to efficiently manage lesson schedules, track daily tasks, and upload homework to receive direct, personalized assistance from their tutors.

The Problem

Students with demanding school routines and their parents often struggle to find qualified, nearby tutors and manage extracurricular lesson schedules efficiently. Traditional methods of seeking help with homework or coordinating with private teachers are often fragmented and time-consuming. There was a clear need for a centralized, informative, and easy-to-navigate platform to streamline the process of finding the right teachers and organizing educational tasks in one place.

Methodology

The development process focused heavily on creating an accessible and intuitive user interface to ensure ease of navigation for users of all ages. A dynamic search and filtering system was implemented to accurately match students with teachers based on proximity and subject expertise. Furthermore, interactive backend functionalities were built to handle the dashboard system, enabling secure homework uploads and organized task management to provide a seamless digital learning experience.
